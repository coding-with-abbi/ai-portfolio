Sam Altman is the CEO of OpenAI, a position he has held since March 2019. Prior to his role at OpenAI, Altman was the president of Y Combinator, a leading startup accelerator. He joined Y Combinator in 2011 as a part-time partner and became its president in February 2014. During his tenure, Y Combinator saw significant growth and launched new initiatives such as YC Continuity, a growth-stage fund, and YC Research, a nonprofit research lab. Altman stepped down from his role at Y Combinator in March 2019 to focus on his responsibilities at OpenAI. He is recognized as a prominent entrepreneur and investor in the tech industry.
