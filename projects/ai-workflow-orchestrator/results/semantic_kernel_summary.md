# Semantic Kernel Summary
## Architecture
Semantic Kernel is part of the Agentic AI systems that can autonomously plan and execute tasks using tools. It is influenced by recent trends such as Multi-Agent Systems (MAS) and Reflection patterns. Key frameworks associated with Semantic Kernel include LangChain, LangGraph, and AutoGen.

## Advantages
Semantic Kernel, as part of the Agentic AI framework, benefits from the ability to autonomously plan and execute tasks, leveraging tools effectively. The integration with trends like Multi-Agent Systems (MAS) and Reflection patterns enhances its adaptability and efficiency.

## Disadvantages
Challenges associated with Semantic Kernel include the risk of infinite loops, high costs, and safety concerns. These are common issues faced by systems within the Agentic AI framework.
